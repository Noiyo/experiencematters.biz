The Critical Moment

Thailand stands at an inflection point. With the highest AI market growth rate in ASEAN (28.55% annually), strong government backing (Thailand 4.0), and strategic geographic position, the country is positioned to become ASEAN's AI hub. Bottom line: 2026-2027 is the critical 18-month window. Organizations and policymakers must act NOW to capture $2.6 trillion economic potential by 2030.
